STEM Academy

STEM at Galena allows students to study in a 4-year course where they learn science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. The 4-year course follows Project Lead The Way's guidelines, which prepare students for college and beyond.

1st Year

Students are introduced to 3D modeling and the engineering design process.

2nd Year

Students are introduced to the math behind many of the world's systems and robot building and coding through VEX.

3rd Year

Students can specialize in Aerospace Engineering or Environmental Sustainability.

4th Year

Students work in groups to research, design, test a solution, and present it to real-world engineers.
